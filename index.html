Frequently Asked Questions

Are Kangsium games free?

Yes — every Kangsium game is free to download and play on Google Play. They are supported by ads, and no purchase is required to enjoy the full game.

Do the games work offline?

Most Kangsium games run completely offline, so you can play on a plane, on the subway, or anywhere without an internet connection.

Do I need to create an account?

No. There are no sign-ups or logins — open a game and start playing right away.

What devices are supported?

Kangsium games are built for Android phones and tablets and are available worldwide on the Google Play Store.

Are the games suitable for children?

Our games are made for a general audience and rated for ages 13 and up. They are simple and family-friendly, though they do display ads.

Email us at jason@kangsium.com with any question, bug report, or piece of feedback — we read every message.
